FAQs

What is UI/UX Design?

UI (User Interface) Design is about how a product looks and feels, while UX (User Experience) Design focuses on the overall feel of the experience.

What tools are commonly used in UI/UX Design?

Popular tools include Figma, Adobe XD, Sketch, InVision, and Photoshop for prototyping and visual design.

What are wireframes and prototypes?

Wireframes are low-fidelity layouts of a page, while prototypes are interactive simulations of a final product design.

Is coding necessary for UI/UX Design?

While not mandatory, basic knowledge of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript can be helpful in understanding the feasibility of designs.
